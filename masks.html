Our Motive


The informal economy workers and urban landless form a majority of those who do menial jobs in the cities. With low and irregular incomes and a lack of social support, many of them are spurred to continue working during the lockdown, often in unsafe conditions, thus exposing themselves and their families to additional risks. Also majority of them lack social protection and access to quality health care making them vulnerable to coronavirus.

CoSafe has taken this initiative to provide these groups with the basic necessities such as face masks, face shields, sanitizers and gloves to work safe in such an environment.
