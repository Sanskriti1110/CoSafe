About Us

CoSafe is a non-profit organization that aims to work particularly on three main areas:

  • Catering unemployment in present Covid-19 scenario.
  • Provide healthcare to people in current situation.
  • Free online education to students in various fields sinceit will be highly valuable for these unemployed young people who are safe in their homes to take this time as an opportunity to learn new, more specific, tech valuable skills.
  • CoSafe has taken this initiative to provide these groups with the basic necessities such as face masks, face shields, sanitizers and gloves to work safe in such an environment.
  • Provides a hotel booking platform in collaboration with many of the hotel industries from all around the world for isolation purposes.
