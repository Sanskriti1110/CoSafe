Covid-19
Dealing with the unforeseen challenges caused by the COVID-19 pandemic has taken a significant toll on people all across the world. Hence CoSafe aims to help as many individuals and families as possible.

Healthcare
CoSafe aims to provide proper healthcare to as many people as possible.

E-Learning
CoSafe provides free online education to students in various fields to help them acquire as much knowledge as possible.
